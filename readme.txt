== Description ==



RevGlue Broadband, TV and Phone connects your wordpress site with RevGlue. It imports the stores, categories and banners you selected at RevGlue to your wordpress website.



Major features in RevGlue Broadband, TV and Phone include:



* Import Stores, banners from your Revglue account. 

* Import Broadband, TV and Phone from your Revglue account. 

* Add your local banners or edit existing from your banners page in WordPress admin dashboard.



PS: You'll need [Subscription key](https://www.revglue.com/) to import Broadband, TV and Phone into your website. There are separate keys for Stores and Banners. You can get a key by registering at RevGlue.com.



== Installation ==



1. Upload the RevGlue Broadband, TV and Phone plugin to your wordpress site and activate it

2. Go to RevGlue Digital->Import Stores and broadband data page and Enter your [ subscription key](https://www.revglue.com/).

3. Go to RevGlue Digital->Import Banners page and Enter your [ banners subscription key](https://www.revglue.com/).



That is it. After verification of your key you can import and view your data.
